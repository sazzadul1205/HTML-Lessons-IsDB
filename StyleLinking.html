Motto of The Emperium OF Man

In the far future of the 41st millennium, there is only war. The galaxy is engulfed in endless conflict where humanity is beset on all sides—by alien horrors, traitorous heretics, and daemonic forces from beyond reality. The Imperium of Man, ruled for ten thousand years by the immortal God-Emperor, survives only through unyielding faith, brutal oppression, and ceaseless sacrifice. There is no peace, no respite, no forgiveness—only the eternal war for survival in a hostile universe.

The Litany of the Machine

The flesh is weakness, soft and fleeting. Steel and circuit are strong, enduring. Trust not in the failing body, but in the sanctity of the Machine. For the Machine is eternal, its spirit immortal. Knowledge is holy, and ignorance is heresy. To seek understanding of the Machine is to seek the Omnissiah. To maintain the Machine is to offer prayer. To build, to repair, to awaken — these are sacred acts. Let the Omnissiah be praised in every turning gear, in every burning circuit, in every strike of the forge. The flesh shall wither, but the Machine endures. Glory to the Omnissiah.
